# Research

Structured research template with web search integration for any topic.

## Instructions

When the user runs `/research [topic]`, conduct systematic research and save findings to a structured document.

## Usage

```
/research AI reasoning models
/research cybersecurity market 2025
/research data infrastructure trends
/research XORQ competitors
```

## Research Process

### 1. **Clarify Scope**
Understand what specifically needs research:
- Market overview?
- Technology deep-dive?
- Competitive landscape?
- Investment opportunity?
- Industry trends?

### 2. **Gather Information**
Use available tools:
- **WebSearch**: Search for recent information, trends, news
- **WebFetch**: Fetch specific articles or company pages
- **Vault Search**: Check existing knowledge in portfolio/notes
- **Grep/Read**: Find related content already in vault

### 3. **Organize Findings**
Structure into actionable sections

### 4. **Link to Portfolio**
Connect research to relevant companies/opportunities

## Research Methods

### **Web Search Strategy**

Use WebSearch tool for:
- Recent news and announcements
- Funding rounds and M&A
- Market reports and analyses
- Technology trends
- Expert opinions and blogs

**Search queries to try:**
- "[Topic] market size 2025"
- "[Topic] funding rounds"
- "[Topic] technology trends"
- "[Topic] key players"
- "[Topic] challenges opportunities"

### **Source Quality Assessment**

Prioritize:
- ‚úÖ Recent (< 6 months for fast-moving topics)
- ‚úÖ Authoritative sources (industry analysts, VCs, practitioners)
- ‚úÖ Data-backed claims
- ‚úÖ Multiple corroborating sources

Flag:
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Vendor marketing (biased)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Old data (outdated)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Single source (unverified)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Speculative without evidence

### **Synthesis Over Summary**

Don't just list findings:
- **Connect dots** across sources
- **Identify patterns** and themes
- **Spot contradictions** and explain
- **Draw conclusions** with reasoning
- **Make recommendations** actionable

## Integration Points

### **Link to Portfolio**
- Check if research affects existing portfolio companies
- Identify competitive threats or opportunities
- Suggest strategic positioning adjustments
- Find potential customer or partnership opportunities

### **Link to Deal Flow**
- Does research reveal investable opportunities?
- White spaces in the market?
- Emerging winners worth tracking?
- Categories to avoid?

### **Link to Network**
After research:
- Run `/find-connections [topic]` to identify experts in your network
- Run `/connect-dots [topic]` to find relevant portfolio/contact connections
- Identify people to talk to for validation

### **Update Thesis**
If research impacts investment thesis:
- Update portfolio company analysis with new insights
- Adjust market size or opportunity assessments
- Flag for discussion in board meetings or LP updates

## Special Research Types

### **Technology Deep-Dive**
Focus on:
- How the technology works
- Technical challenges and limitations
- Innovation vectors
- Adoption barriers
- Competitive approaches

### **Market Opportunity**
Focus on:
- TAM/SAM/SOM analysis
- Customer segments and needs
- Go-to-market strategies
- Unit economics
- Growth trajectories

### **Competitive Intelligence**
Focus on:
- Who's doing what
- Funding and traction
- Positioning and messaging
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Strategic moves

### **Trend Analysis**
Focus on:
- What's changing and why
- Drivers of change
- Timeline and velocity
- Winners and losers
- Implications for strategy

## Important Notes

- Save all research to `Research/` folder with date in filename
- Link to portfolio companies using `[[WikiLinks]]`
- Include sources for credibility and follow-up
- Be honest about confidence levels
- Make findings actionable, not just informational
- Update research periodically for fast-moving topics
- Cross-reference with vault: might already have insights
- Tag with relevant categories for future findability
- End with clear next steps - research should drive action

## After Research Completed

**Share Insights:**
- Relevant portfolio companies
- Team members or advisors
- LPs (if appropriate)

**Apply Learnings:**
- Update portfolio company strategies
- Adjust investment thesis
- Identify deal flow opportunities
- Inform board discussions

**Track Evolution:**
- Set reminder to update research
- Monitor for new developments
- Compare predictions to reality
