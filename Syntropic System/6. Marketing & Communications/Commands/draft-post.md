# Draft Post

Generate LinkedIn posts with your authentic voice - executive perspective, portfolio insights, thought leadership.

## Instructions

When the user runs `/draft-post [topic]`, create a polished LinkedIn post ready to publish.

## Usage

```
/draft-post agentic AI security trends
/draft-post lessons from portfolio company journey
/draft-post why I'm excited about data infrastructure
/draft-post [any topic or idea]
```

## Voice & Style Guidelines

**Your Voice:**
- Executive/investor perspective (not salesy)
- Thoughtful, analytical, evidence-based
- Humble expertise (share insights, not ego)
- Action-oriented (what can others learn?)
- Portfolio-informed but not promotional

**Structure:**
- **Hook** (1-2 sentences that grab attention)
- **Insight** (2-4 paragraphs of substance)
- **Takeaway** (what should reader do/think?)
- **Optional:** Portfolio connection if relevant

**Length:**
- Aim for 150-250 words
- 3-5 paragraphs
- LinkedIn sweet spot (readable but substantive)

**Formatting:**
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use line breaks generously
- Occasional emoji (sparingly, strategically)
- Bold for emphasis on key points

## Post Process

### 1. **Understand Topic**
- What's the core idea?
- Why does it matter?
- What's the insight others might miss?

### 2. **Research Context**
- Check vault for related notes
- Look for portfolio company connections
- Find data/evidence to support points
- Identify contrarian or non-obvious angle

### 3. **Craft Hook**
Strong opening options:
- Surprising data point
- Contrarian take
- Personal observation
- Question that makes reader think
- Pattern you've noticed

### 4. **Develop Insight**
- Lead with thesis
- Support with evidence (data, examples, portfolio learnings)
- Anticipate objections
- Add nuance (avoid oversimplification)

### 5. **Close with Takeaway**
- What should reader remember?
- What action can they take?
- What question should they consider?
- Optional: Invite discussion

## Content Types & Approaches

### **1. Market Analysis Post**
Format:
- Hook: Surprising market data or trend
- Insight: What it means, why it matters
- Implication: How founders/investors should think about it
- Close: Question or prediction

Example hook:
"The agentic AI security market just hit an inflection point. Here's what 15 funding rounds in 60 days tells us..."

### **2. Portfolio Lesson Post**
Format:
- Hook: Specific situation from portfolio work
- Challenge: What made it hard/interesting
- Insight: What you learned (generalizable)
- Takeaway: How others can apply it

Example hook:
"Watching a portfolio company navigate a pivot taught me more about product-market fit than any framework ever could."

### **3. Contrarian Take Post**
Format:
- Hook: State the conventional wisdom
- Challenge: Why it's incomplete or wrong
- Evidence: Data/examples supporting your view
- Nuance: Acknowledge when conventional wisdom does apply

Example hook:
"Everyone says 'move fast and break things.' But I've seen more startups die from speed than slowness. Here's why..."

### **4. Trend Spotting Post**
Format:
- Hook: Pattern you're seeing (3+ data points)
- Analysis: Why it's happening now
- Prediction: Where it's headed
- Action: What to do about it

Example hook:
"Three portfolio companies just got the same customer objection. This isn't coincidenceâ€”it's a market shift."

### **5. Question/Discussion Post**
Format:
- Hook: Provocative question
- Context: Why you're asking (what prompted it)
- Your take: Your current thinking (not definitive)
- Invitation: Genuinely want others' perspectives

Example hook:
"How do you know when to double down vs. when to pivot? I'm wrestling with this for two portfolio companies right now."

### **6. Data-Driven Post**
Format:
- Hook: Surprising statistic or research finding
- Context: Where it comes from, why credible
- Interpretation: What it actually means (avoid obvious)
- Application: How to use this insight

Example hook:
"Only 12% of AI security companies focus on agentic systems. Yet 78% of security leaders say it's their top concern. The disconnect is massive."

### **7. Resource Share Post**
Format:
- Hook: Why this resource matters
- Summary: Key takeaways (save reader time)
- Application: How you're using it
- Link: Where to find it

Example hook:
"This market map changed how I evaluate deals in the data infrastructure space. Three insights that stuck with me..."

## Research & Validation

### **Before writing:**

**Check vault for:**
- Portfolio company notes (relevant learnings?)
- Research documents (supporting data?)
- Contact notes (interesting conversations?)
- Consulting projects (applicable lessons?)

**Web search for:**
- Recent news/data on topic
- What others are saying (to differentiate)
- Data to support your points
- Validate your thesis isn't outdated

**Consider:**
- Is this insight actually valuable?
- Am I the right person to share this?
- Is timing right for this topic?
- Who's the audience for this?

## Tips for Great Posts

1. **Start with reader benefit:** Why should they care?
2. **Be specific:** "3 companies" beats "many companies"
3. **Show, don't tell:** Examples > assertions
4. **Respect reader's time:** Get to the point, then support it
5. **End with action:** What should reader think/do/consider?
6. **Use portfolio ethically:** Share learnings, not confidential info
7. **Iterate:** First draft rarely bestâ€”generate multiple versions
8. **Test hooks:** Try 3-5 different openings, pick strongest
9. **Read aloud:** If it doesn't sound like you speaking, revise
10. **Post regularly:** Algorithm and audience reward consistency
